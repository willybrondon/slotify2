Frequently Asked Questions

How to create a customer account on Slotify?

  1. Download the Slotify Customer App by scanning the QR code above.
  2. Open the app and tap Sign Up.
  3. Fill in your details and follow the on-screen instructions.
  4. Verify your phone number or email as prompted.

How to create a salon owner account?

  1. Download the Slotify Customer App and sign up as a customer first.
  2. Go to the Profile section and select Register your salon.
  3. Fill in your salon details and specify the number of experts (staff) you want to add.
  4. Once approved, you will receive credentials for your expert(s).

How does an expert get their credentials?

The salon owner creates expert accounts during salon registration. Each expert receives their login credentials from the salon owner.
