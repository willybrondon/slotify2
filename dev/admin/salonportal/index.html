Frequently Asked Questions

How does SalonPortal work for my clients?

After creating your personalized salon page on the booking portal, your clients can book your services in a few clicks, according to your available slots. No more phone interruptions, appointments are managed autonomously, always according to your constraints.

Will SalonPortal bring me new clients?

Your salon will be visible to our 10 million monthly users and listed on search networks like Google to easily find your establishment. Verified reviews from your previous appointments will encourage visitors to book simply, without fees.

How much does the SalonPortal subscription cost?

SalonPortal offers no-commitment subscriptions at fixed and transparent prices: no commission on your appointments, no installation or maintenance fees. Contact one of our advisors who will guide you to the offer best suited to your needs.

Will the SalonPortal subscription be profitable for my salon?

SalonPortal features increase client returns by 60% monthly while reducing missed appointments by 75%. Your salon page will attract 5 new clients monthly on average. Your schedule is filled to maximize your revenue.
